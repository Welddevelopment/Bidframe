AcmeClean Ltd — Company Profile

AcmeClean Ltd is a commercial cleaning contractor established in 2009, providing office,
healthcare and public-sector cleaning services across Scotland.

Accreditations: AcmeClean Ltd holds ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management), certificate number
GB-2009-44213, issued by BSI. We also hold ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management).

Insurance: We carry Employer's Liability Insurance of GBP 10,000,000 and Public Liability
Insurance of GBP 5,000,000, both maintained for the full duration of every contract.

Account management: Every contract is assigned a dedicated account manager as the single point
of contact for the client, supported by a regional operations supervisor.
