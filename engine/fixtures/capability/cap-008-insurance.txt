AcmeClean Ltd — Insurance Cover

AcmeClean Ltd maintains the following insurance cover for the full duration of every contract, and
can provide copies of the current certificates to the preferred bidder stage on request:

Employer's Liability Insurance: GBP 10,000,000 (ten million pounds) per occurrence, as required by
law for all our employees.

Public Liability Insurance: GBP 5,000,000 (five million pounds) per occurrence, covering injury to
third parties and damage to client property.

Professional Indemnity Insurance: GBP 1,000,000 (one million pounds) in the aggregate.

These policies are placed with a leading insurer rated by a recognised agency and are renewed
annually. The organisation's professional indemnity or public liability insurance certificate can be
supplied before the award of the contract, at the preferred bidder stage, on request.
