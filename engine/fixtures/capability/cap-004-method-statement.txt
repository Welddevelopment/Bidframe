AcmeClean Ltd — Cleaning Method Statement (Standard Office Service)

This method statement describes AcmeClean Ltd's standard approach to routine commercial office work.
A colour-coded microfibre cloth and mop system is applied throughout to prevent cross-contamination
between areas. Frequencies are agreed with each client and issued as a written schedule, which our
regional supervisor signs off against on each visit.

Our operatives vacuum all carpeted and hard floor areas, paying attention to edges and under desks,
and spot clean carpet stains with pro-clean as required.

Wastepaper bins are emptied and bin liners replenished on every visit, with waste taken to the
disposal point.

This standard office service covers reception, open-plan and meeting-room work. Desk-top and surface
detailing, kitchen deep cleaning, washroom sanitising routines and grounds tasks are detailed
separately in a site-specific specification agreed with the client after a survey; they fall outside
this generic method statement.
