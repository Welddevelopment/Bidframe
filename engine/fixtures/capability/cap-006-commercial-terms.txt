AcmeClean Ltd — Commercial and Contract Terms

Pricing model: AcmeClean Ltd prices office cleaning contracts on a cost per person per hour basis,
plus a separately itemised charge for consumables (janitorial materials, bin liners, toilet paper,
soaps and cleaning chemicals). Our bid pricing sets out the cost per person per hour, the
consumables charge, and the total cost of the contract including VAT at the prevailing rate. A full
cost breakdown is provided as a priced schedule with our bid.

Price review: Our quoted prices are held firm for the duration of the initial contract period. Where
costs are not fixed for the full duration of the contract, the review points and the basis of the
review (for example an agreed annual uplift linked to a published index) are clearly detailed in our
pricing schedule, giving the client full visibility of how and when prices are adjusted.

Payment periods: AcmeClean Ltd invoices on a monthly basis in arrears. Our standard payment position
is a monthly invoice with payment due within 30 days of the invoice date. We can align invoice and
payment periods with the client's own accounts-payable schedule where needed.

Termination: The standard terms of our cleaning contracts allow either party to terminate on 90
days' written notice, with immediate termination available for material breach that is left
unremedied. The full termination provisions and the standard terms of the contract are set out in
our standard terms and conditions, which form part of the contractual relationship with the client
and are issued with our bid.

Bid costs: AcmeClean Ltd meets its own costs of preparing and submitting bids, and absorbs all
bid-preparation expenses rather than passing them to the client.
