AcmeClean Ltd — Quality Assurance and Audit

Quality assurance checks: AcmeClean Ltd carries out documented quality assurance checks of all work
done and keeps the records for audit. Our regional supervisor completes a scored inspection against
the agreed task schedule on each site visit, and the client receives a copy of the inspection record.

Records and audit trail: Quality assurance inspection records, COSHH files and risk assessments are
retained for the duration of the contract and are available to the client for audit at any time. Any
issue identified at an inspection is logged, actioned and signed off, with the record kept on file.

ISO 9001 quality management: Our quality assurance procedure is aligned with, and audited under, our
ISO 9001:2015 quality management system (certificate GB-2009-44213, issued by BSI). Internal audits
and an annual external surveillance audit verify that the procedure is being followed on every site.

Continuous improvement: Client feedback and inspection scores are reviewed by the account manager at
a regular contract review meeting, and corrective actions are tracked to completion so that the
quality of the service is maintained throughout the contract.
