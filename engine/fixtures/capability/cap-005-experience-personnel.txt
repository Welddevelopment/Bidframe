AcmeClean Ltd — Experience and Proposed Personnel

Experience of similar work: AcmeClean Ltd has fifteen years' experience delivering office and
public-sector cleaning contracts of comparable size and scope to this requirement. We currently hold
eleven live commercial contracts across central Scotland, including multi-storey office buildings,
healthcare premises and listed public buildings, and we have a demonstrable track record of
mobilising new contracts within four weeks of award.

Proposed personnel: For a contract of this size we propose a team of two trained cleaning operatives
per visit, led by a working supervisor, with cover arranged for holidays and absence so that service
is never interrupted. Every operative is fully trained in our colour-coded system, COSHH handling
and health and safety procedures before starting on site, and holds an enhanced DBS check where the
contract requires it.

Personnel and TUPE: When a contract transfers between providers, AcmeClean Ltd honours its TUPE
obligations and engages constructively with transferring staff. A dedicated account manager is
assigned to the contract as the single point of contact for the client, supported by the regional
operations supervisor who carries out the routine site inspections.

Similar work and relevant experience: The experience and proposed personnel set out here relate
directly to similar office-cleaning work, and further detail of comparable contracts and the
personnel proposed to service this contract is provided with our two client references.
