AcmeClean Ltd — Health, Safety and COSHH Policy

Risk assessment and COSHH: AcmeClean Ltd maintains up-to-date risk assessments and COSHH manuals for
every site. The site risk assessment and COSHH manual is kept on site at all times and is reviewed
quarterly, and after any change to the products or tasks, through the account manager. Every cleaning
chemical we use is listed with its safety data sheet in the site COSHH file.

Safe handling of chemicals: All operatives are trained in the safe handling, dilution, storage and
disposal of cleaning chemicals before starting on site. Colour-coded equipment and well-labelled,
correctly diluted products are used to control the risks identified in each risk assessment.

Health and safety management: We operate a documented health and safety policy covering manual
handling, slips, trips and falls, working at height and lone working. Method statements and risk
assessments are provided for any non-routine task. Our health and safety arrangements are consistent
with our ISO 9001 quality management system and are audited internally.

Certification and standards: AcmeClean Ltd holds ISO 9001:2015 for quality management and ISO
14001:2015 for environmental management, and our COSHH and risk-assessment procedures are maintained
as part of that certified management system.
