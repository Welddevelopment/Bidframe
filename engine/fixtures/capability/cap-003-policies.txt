AcmeClean Ltd — Policies and Procedures

Health and Safety: We maintain up-to-date risk assessments and COSHH manuals on every site, kept
on site and reviewed quarterly by the account manager.

Quality Assurance: We carry out documented quality assurance inspections of all work and retain
the records for audit. Our QA procedure is aligned with our ISO 9001 quality management system.

Confidentiality: All staff are bound by confidentiality agreements and are DBS-checked where the
contract requires it.
